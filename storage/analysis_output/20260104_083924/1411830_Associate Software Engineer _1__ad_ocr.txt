Associate Software Engineer
We are looking for an Associate Software Engineer to join our growing IT team. In this role, you will
contribute to the development, maintenance, and optimization of internal software systems, ensuring
they’re reliable, secure, and seamlessly integrated with existing platforms.
Key Responsibilities
+ Design, develop, maintain, and improve software solutions to support internal departments.
+ Ensure proper use of version control systems and maintain accurate technical documentation
and change logs.
+ Follow secure coding practices and ensure all systems adhere to IT security standards.
+ Assist in integrating new solutions with existing platforms such as ERP, CMS, and web portals.
+ Manage and respond to internal IT helpdesk tickets, providing timely and effective technical
support.
Skills and Experience Required
+ Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
+ Basic understanding of enterprise software applications and business workflows.
+ Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
+ Proficiency in programming languages such as PHP and JavaScript.
+ Basic knowledge of databases and SQL.
+ Ability to write clean, efficient, and well-documented code.
+ Willingness to learn new technologies and tools in a fast-paced environment.
+ Understanding of basic software testing principles.
+ Ability to follow established coding standards and best practices.
The successful candidate would be provided with a competitive remuneration package
on par with industry standards and other fringe benefits
