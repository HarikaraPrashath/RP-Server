HEAD OF OPERATIONAL EXCELLENCE - COLOMBo
Position Overview:
The Head of Operational Excellence will be a high-level strategic leader responsible for steering continuous improvement initiatives across all factory and support operations. This role requires a visionary approach to operational design, process improvement, technological innovation, and adaptive manufacturing models based on macroeconomic dynamics. Additionally, the position encompasses oversight of industrial engineering activities including work studies, method optimization, capacity planning, and facility layout to maximize operational efficiency and flexibility.
Key Responsibilities:
Strategic & Operational Leadership:
Formulate and implement a comprehensive Operational Excellence strategy aligned with the Company’s international growth, diversification, and resilience goals. Develop adaptable operating models that respond swiftly to macroeconomic shifts such as currency fluctuations, inflation, or supply chain disruptions.
Process Optimization & Continuous Improvement:
Lead deployment of LEAN, Six Sigma, Kaizen, and other continuous improvement methodologies. Focus on waste elimination, process standardization, yield optimization, and operational agility across all manufacturing sites.
Industrial Engineering & Work Studies:
Oversee all industrial engineering activities, including work measurement, method study, time study, line balancing, facility layout optimization, and capacity analysis. Implement work standardization and ergonomic improvements to enhance productivity and safety.
Digital & Technological Transformation:
Accelerate adoption of Industry 4.0, automation, IoT, and data analytics to improve efficiency, predictive maintenance, and decision-making. Ensure manufacturing systems are flexible enough to adapt to shifting economic and market conditions.
Evolving Operating Models & Macro-economic Responsiveness:
Develop and refine flexible operating frameworks that adjust to macroeconomic variables. Conduct scenario planning and economic modeling to optimize capacity, costs, and supply chain under different regional and global conditions.
Supporting International & New Facility Projects:
Collaborate with cross-functional teams in setting up new manufacturing facilities abroad, ensuring operational standards, scalability, and flexibility tailored to local market conditions.
Product Diversification & High-Margin Development:
Enable production processes for new high-margin non-dairy products. Support innovation pipelines with process engineering and operational scalability.
Contract Manufacturing & White Label Operations:
Develop standards, KPIs, and oversight mechanisms for third-party manufacturing and white-labeled products. Optimize costs, quality, and logistics integration for seamless third-party operations.
Supply Chain & Capacity Planning:
Identify bottlenecks, optimize inventory levels, and develop scalable supply chain models resilient to macroeconomic risks. Use industrial engineering tools for capacity planning and bottleneck analysis.
Performance Monitoring & Data-Driven Decision Making:
Establish KPIs related to safety, quality, efficiency, energy, and sustainability. Utilize work studies, time analysis, and process audits for continuous improvement.
Change Management & Culture Development:
Lead initiatives to embed a culture of operational excellence, agility, and innovation. Provide training, mentorship, and leadership to operational teams.
Compliance & Sustainability:
Ensure all manufacturing activities adhere to global standards (ISO, HACCP, FDA) and promote eco-friendly practices to meet sustainability goals.
Leadership & Stakeholder Engagement:
Direct a skilled team of industrial engineers, quality professionals, and operations managers. Foster collaboration across departments to align operational goals with corporate strategy.
Industry & Market Insights:
Keep abreast of global industry trends, technological innovations, and macroeconomic factors, applying insights to optimize manufacturing strategies.
